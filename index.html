A Swingin' Good Time.

We provide a fun and safe place for all Youngstown State University students to learn, practice, and enjoy the art of swing dancing.

Our meetings are Tuesdays at 6pm in the upper deck of Beeghly Center, room 325!

Learn more »

Learn.

At the beginning of each meeting, we provide a lesson structured such that members with all levels of dance experience will come away with new knowledge. Whether it's your first time dancing or you've been dancing for years, you are sure to pick up something new.

Practice.

After the lesson, each meeting features social dancing where you can put everything you just learned into practice. Get out on the dance floor and have a great time swing dancing with fellow students.

Grow.

We encourage all members to share feedback with each other during the social dance portion of our meetings. Through practicing and communicating with your fellow students, you will grow as a dancer.
